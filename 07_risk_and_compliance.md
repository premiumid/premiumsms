# Risk and Compliance Notes

## Business Risks

- Provider outages.
- Chargebacks and wallet abuse.
- Fraudulent signups.
- SMS pumping or spam abuse.
- Account bans from external services.

## Technical Controls

- Rate limits.
- IP reputation checks.
- Email and phone verification for accounts.
- Manual review for suspicious behavior.
- Strong audit logs.

## Operational Controls

- Provider fallback list.
- Refund policy.
- Support escalation workflow.
- Balance reconciliation every day.
- Admin alerting for downtime.

## Data Protection

- Encrypt sensitive data.
- Hash API keys.
- Limit staff access.
- Keep logs minimal.
- Separate customer data from operational keys.
